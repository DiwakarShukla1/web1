Our Founder

hri. S.N. Ranade is known as the "Father of the Micronutrient Industry in India." He founded the Institute for Micronutrient Technology (IMT) in 1967 when the concept of Balanced Plant Nutrition(BPN) was vitually unknown and use of NPK fertilizers was still in its infancy. He gave the Institute our top-selling.

MICNELF and BORACOL, both of which have been on the market since 1976. Shri S. N. Ranade retired from active work in 1990 and passed away peacefully in 1996.

Our Chairman

Dr. Shrikant S. Ranade joined Mr. S. N. Ranade in 1973 and has been an integral part of the company since then.

Under his leadership IMT has expanded both geographically and operationally. Besides technical and research development, IMT has broadened the scope by setting up high quality control standards for micronutrient mixture fertilisers and analytical service standards for the agricultural sector. Today, the Institute and associate companies are authorities in the field of Balanced Plant Nutrition in India and Abroad.

Our Group Companies

IMT, in the last three decades, has redefined the sustainable use of fertilisers and many progressive farmers have reinforced their existing cultural practices by incorporating BPN principles.

We draw our technical strength from the agricultural universities in India and our corporate members have agro - related specialization in various fields. With strong technical and scientific training, we are well -versed in the field of Plant Nutrition and have the confidence to produce and promote technically sound products.

Corporate Objectives

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

Research and analyze nutrient requirements of crops based on soil and crop characteristics

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Develop products based on the nutrient requirements of crops grown under varied soil and environmental conditions

TECHNOLOGY DISSIMINATION

Provide the agricultural community with knowledge of BPN in general and Micronutrients in particular

Corporate Objectives

To the status of Micronutrients in India

IMT has been instrumental in including micronutrients in the Fertiliser Control Order of the Ministry of Agriculture of India in 1985.

To the Scientific Community

A public charitable trust - ' Shobhana Shrikrishna Ranade Memorial Trust' was started in 1995 in memory of the wife of Mr. S. N. Ranade. Through this trust, IMT has instituted awards for excellence in micronutrient research. Each award carries a monetary component with a Certificate of Excellence and a silver memento.
Since 1997, we have annually granted awards for the following categories:

  • Lifetime Achievement Award
  • Junior Scientist Award
  • Senior Scientist Award
  • Best Thesis Award

To the Agricultural Community

IMT strongly believes in the information - transfer of science and technology.

  • Various technical publications authored by company scientists are distributed free of cost to the farmers and technical field officers.
  • A technical news bullet in, IMT NEWS, notifies the farmer / technical personnel of current product developments at the Institute & product / crop recommendations.
  • We also provide progressive farmers with a chat Forum on our web site.

We conduct short - term training programs on Principles of Balanced Plant Nutrition.

Technical guidance and crop managements services are provided free of cost and analytical services are provided at affordable prices.
